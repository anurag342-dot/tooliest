How Do I Add Page Numbers to a PDF?

Add page numbers to PDF files with custom position, format, font size, and starting number.

When pagination helps
Page numbers make long PDFs easier to review, reference, print, and discuss during approvals, edits, or classroom handouts.

Practical Examples & Benchmarks

  • PDF Page Numbers is most useful when you need a quick answer or transformation without pausing to open a larger app or a slower manual workflow.
  • Browser-based tools are especially handy for short tasks, rapid checks, and situations where you want to copy the result immediately and keep moving.

How to number PDF pages online

  1. Upload your PDF - Open the document that needs page numbers added to the top or bottom of each page.
  2. Choose the numbering format - Set the starting number, page position, and display format that matches your document.
  3. Apply the numbering - Run the pagination step to place the numbers on every page in the file.
  4. Download the numbered PDF - Save the updated PDF with the new page numbering already applied.

Why Use PDF Page Numbers?

  • Add page numbering to reports, ebooks, packets, and handouts
  • Choose the numbering format, starting number, and page position
  • Generate numbered PDFs without desktop software or uploads

Who Uses PDF Page Numbers?

Teachers, agencies, proposal writers, and office teams who need cleaner reference-ready documents.
