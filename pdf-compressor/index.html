How Does PDF Compression Work?

PDF compression reduces file size by downsampling embedded images, removing duplicate objects, and applying deflate compression to streams. This tool processes everything in your browser — your PDF never leaves your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I reduce a PDF's file size?

Typical compression reduces file size by 30-80% depending on the PDF's content. PDFs with many high-resolution images see the biggest reductions.

Will compression affect text quality?

No. Text and vector graphics remain crisp. Only embedded raster images are resampled, and you control the quality level.
