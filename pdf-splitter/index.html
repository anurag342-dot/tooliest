How Do I Split PDF Files Online Without Uploading Them?

Split PDF files by page range, extract selected pages, or create a file every N pages.

What PDF splitting is used for
Splitting lets you turn one long PDF into smaller deliverables, such as a single invoice, a chapter, a signature page, or one file per section.

How to split a PDF into smaller files

  1. Upload one PDF - Choose the PDF you want to split by range, every N pages, or a custom page selection.
  2. Choose a split mode - Pick whether you want separate files by range, one file every N pages, or one file per page.
  3. Enter the pages to export - Provide the page numbers or ranges you want to keep in each output file.
  4. Split and download - Generate the new PDFs and download the results directly from your browser.

Why Use PDF Splitter?

  • Extract only the pages you need from a longer document
  • Split large PDFs into smaller files for email or review workflows
  • Use range, specific-page, or every-N-pages modes in one place

Who Uses PDF Splitter?

Legal teams, finance teams, teachers, and document-heavy operations teams who need tighter control over page ranges.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I split a PDF into individual pages?

Yes. PDF Splitter can export each page as its own PDF or create grouped files from custom ranges.

How do I extract only a few pages from a PDF?

Upload the file, choose the extract or custom-range mode, enter the page numbers you want, and download the smaller result.
