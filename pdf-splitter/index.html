How Do I Split a PDF Online?

Upload your PDF, choose how you want to split it (by page range, every N pages, extract specific pages, or each page separately), and click Split. All processing is done in your browser — your files never leave your device.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Upload a PDF — Drag and drop or browse to select your PDF file.
  2. Choose split mode — Select range, every N pages, extract specific, or each page.
  3. Configure settings — Enter page numbers or ranges as needed.
  4. Split and download — Click Split PDF and download the resulting files.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I split a PDF into separate pages?

Upload your PDF, select "Each Page" mode, and click Split. Each page becomes its own PDF file that you can download individually.

Can I extract specific pages from a PDF?

Yes. Use the "Extract Pages" mode and enter the page numbers (e.g., 1, 3, 5-8). The tool creates a new PDF containing only those pages.
