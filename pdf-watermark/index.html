How Do I Add a Watermark to a PDF Online?

Add text watermarks to every page of a PDF with adjustable angle, opacity, color, and size.

Why watermarks are used
Watermarks help label drafts, confidential files, approvals, and internal copies so recipients can identify document status at a glance.

How to watermark every page of a PDF

  1. Upload the PDF - Open the document that needs a watermark applied to every page.
  2. Enter the watermark text - Type the label you want to apply, such as Draft, Confidential, or Approved.
  3. Adjust the styling - Choose the watermark angle, opacity, size, and color before you export the final file.
  4. Export the watermarked PDF - Generate the updated PDF and download it directly from your browser.

Why Use PDF Watermark?

  • Stamp every PDF page with draft, confidential, approved, or custom text
  • Control watermark angle, color, opacity, and size before export
  • Protect privacy because the source file never leaves your browser

Who Uses PDF Watermark?

Sales teams, legal teams, HR, agencies, and operations teams who circulate internal or pre-release documents.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I watermark every page in a PDF at once?

Yes. PDF Watermark applies the same text watermark across the entire document in one browser-based pass.

Will the watermark become part of the PDF permanently?

Yes. The exported file includes the watermark on the pages, so it stays with the PDF you download.
