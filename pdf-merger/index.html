How Do I Merge PDF Files Online for Free?

Merge multiple PDF files into one document with drag-and-drop page ordering and thumbnail previews.

How PDF merging works
PDF merging copies pages from several source files into a brand-new PDF container so you can combine contracts, reports, invoices, and scanned packets without installing desktop software.

How to merge PDF files step by step

  1. Upload your PDF files - Add two or more PDF files from your device to start building the merged document.
  2. Reorder or remove pages - Use the thumbnail grid to drag pages into the final order and remove anything you do not want to keep.
  3. Merge everything into one file - Run the merge action to combine all selected pages into one new PDF.
  4. Download the merged PDF - Save the finished file locally and share it without sending source documents to a server.

Why Use PDF Merger?

  • Combine multiple PDF files in one browser-based flow without uploads
  • Reorder pages visually before saving the final document
  • Keep sensitive PDFs private because the merge happens on your device

Who Uses PDF Merger?

Operations teams, students, recruiters, accountants, and anyone who needs to assemble several PDFs into one clean document.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I combine multiple PDF files into one?

Upload the files, arrange the page order, and export one merged PDF. Tooliest combines the pages locally in your browser.

Can I rearrange PDF pages before merging?

Yes. PDF Merger includes page thumbnails, so you can drag pages into the exact order you want before downloading the final file.

Is PDF merging private on Tooliest?

Yes. The merge happens on your device in the browser, so your PDFs are not uploaded to a remote server.
