FAQ

Can I rotate individual pages?

Yes. Hover over any page thumbnail and click the rotation button to rotate just that page. Other pages remain unchanged.

Does rotation affect text quality?

No. Rotation adjusts the PDF page metadata without re-rendering content. Text, images, and vectors remain pixel-perfect.
